Key Features

01
Barcode POS
Scan barcodes or search by name to add products to a ticket. Filter by category — Jackets, Shirts, Shoes, Accessories, Pants — and sell in seconds.
02
Multi-Variant Inventory
Each product supports multiple variants (sizes, colors). Track stock per variant, flag low or out-of-stock items, and import via CSV.
03
Sales & Profit Tracking
Full transaction history with unique codes, staff attribution, payment method, status (Paid / Voided), and date filtering. Gross profit calculated automatically.
04
Receipt Printing
Configurable receipt width (80mm / 58mm) with store name, address, phone, and NIF/Tax ID — ready for any thermal printer.
05
Staff Accounts
Multiple staff accounts with Admin and Staff roles. Sales are attributed to each staff member, with a Top Staff leaderboard on the dashboard.
06
Live Dashboard
Today, this week, and this month revenue at a glance. Recent sales feed, top categories, and 30-day sales chart — all updating in real time.
