Key Features

📡
NFC Check-in
Members tap their NFC tag to instantly identify themselves. Manual tag ID entry as fallback.
👥
Member Profiles
Track subscriptions, expiry dates, phone numbers, and NFC tag assignments per member.
📦
Inventory
Manage supplement stock in grams. Sell, restock, and edit items with per-100g pricing.
💰
POS Sales
Quick sales with configurable price presets. Full sales history with revenue totals in DZD.
📊
Daily Analytics
Per-user daily breakdown of POS transactions, revenue, and NFC check-in counts.
⚙️
Multi-Account
Multiple staff accounts (Admin, badro1) with independent activity tracking.
