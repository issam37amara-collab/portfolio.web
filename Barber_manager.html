Key Features
🪑
Live Queue
Reception adds clients to any barber's queue in real time. Walk-ins or named clients, handled instantly.
✂️
Barber Panel
Each barber gets their own panel to view their current client, select services, and start or finish sessions.
💳
Point of Sale
Pending payments queue with itemized order details. One click to confirm payment and close the order.
📋
Service Catalog
Add, edit, or remove services and prices anytime. Changes reflect across all barber panels immediately.
📊
Revenue Dashboard
Total revenue, clients, and orders with per-barber breakdown and a weekly orders chart — filterable by date range.
👤
Multi-Role Access
Admin, Barber, and Reception roles. Each user sees only what they need — no clutter, no confusion.
